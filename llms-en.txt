# iirest

> iirest is a web SaaS for restaurant procurement teams. It helps compare supplier prices, build orders, and track savings.

Canonical: https://iirest.ru/
Language: en
Product type: B2B SoftwareApplication
Russian source: https://iirest.ru/llms.txt

## What iirest does

- Compares supplier prices in one matrix view.
- Speeds up procurement order preparation.
- Supports price-list uploads and supplier workflows.
- Shows analytics for savings, overpayment, and purchasing dynamics.

## Primary audience

- Restaurant owners and managers.
- Procurement specialists and purchasing teams.
- Multi-location horeca operators.
